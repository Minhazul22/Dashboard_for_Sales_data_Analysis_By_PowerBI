## 📌 Overview
This project presents an **interactive Sales Dashboard** built in **Power BI** to analyze e-commerce performance across multiple dimensions including profit, sales, customers, and geography.

The dashboard is designed to help businesses **identify trends, optimize decisions, and improve profitability** through data-driven insights.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall sales and profit performance  
- Identify high-performing product categories  
- Understand customer purchasing behavior  
- Evaluate regional sales distribution  
- Examine preferred payment methods

## 📈 Dashboard Insights

### 🔹 Profit Analysis
- Monthly profit trends highlight seasonal performance patterns  
- Sub-category analysis reveals top profit drivers like **Printers** and **Sarees**

### 🔹 Sales Distribution
- Clothing dominates with **63% of total quantity sold**  
- Electronics and Furniture contribute smaller but significant shares  

### 🔹 Payment Behavior
- **Cash on Delivery (44%)** is the most preferred method  
- Digital payments (UPI, Cards) show strong adoption  

### 🔹 Regional Performance
- Top contributing states:
  - Maharashtra  
  - Madhya Pradesh  
  - Uttar Pradesh  
  - Gujarat  
  - Andhra Pradesh  

### 🔹 Customer Insights
- Identifies high-value customers contributing most to revenue  
- Enables targeted marketing strategies
